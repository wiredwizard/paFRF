Our Services

Sometimes it isn’t about the monetary benefit, but the show of moral support and the feeling of not being alone.

Help comes in many forms. From cutting up a winter’s worth of wood for a rider and his family, helping a family remain close to their parents when they went down by providing lodging in a place close to the hospital. We often provide gas cards and/or food cards to get to and from the hospitals and grab a bite to eat while there. We organize rides to visit fallen riders at various locations to show support and encouragement.

Donations are gratefully accepted through Amazon Smile where Amazon donates 0.5% of the price of eligible AmazonSmile purchases. Log into your Amazon account at smile.amazon.com and choose Fallen Riders Foundation as your Charity.

Donations are also accepted through PayPal! We are funded 100% by Public Donations.

Education

Motorcyclist deaths occurred 27 times more frequently than fatalities in other vehicles, based on fatal crash data. To keep motorcyclists safe, we urge everyone to share the road and be alert, and we're reminding motorcyclists to review and have the following documents prepared.

  • FRF Rider Information Card
  • Health Care Proxy (POA)
  • Power of Attorney
  • Rights of Succession
  • Living Will
